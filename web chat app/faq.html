FAQ

What does the T in BabbleT stand for?

The T in BabbleT stands for text.

How do I register?

In the homepage, you can click on the Chat button in the menu.
It will pop up a Login and Registration button. Clicking the Registration button will take you to the Registration form.
Create a username and password and fill in the form.
Click submit when you are done. A new account will be created for you.
You can then login to begin chatting.

How do I login?

In the homepage, you can click on the Chat button in the menu.
It will pop up a Login and Registration button.
Clicking the Login button will take you to the Login form.
Enter you username and password. Click submit when you are done.
You will be taken to the main page where you can begin chatting.

How do I search a topic?

Enter your desired topic in the search box.
Click search when you are done.
A list of matched users will appear in a table.

How do I join a chat room?

After searching for a topic, you can select a user from the matched users table.
The web application will automatically create the chat interface for you.
You can then start chatting with the selected user.

How do I logout?

You can logout by clicking the Logout button above the search box.
You will be taken back to the login page.

What are the chat room policies?

There are no particular room policies. You are free to chat whatever you want.
However, you must comply to law of your country.

Is it mobile compatible?

Our website is mobile compatible.
You can chat on a tablet, ipod, iPhone, Androids, Blackberry, Windows phone and etc.

Is there a multi-user chat room?

There is currently no multi-user chat room.

How to leave or quit chat room?

You can leave or quit chat room by clicking the Quit button above the chat box.
You can then search another topic.

How to send message?

Enter your message in the message box below the chat window.
Click Send to send your message.

Is there minimum age restriction to join?

The minimum age to join BabbleT is 14 years old.

How do I select a user to chat with?

When you are displayed with a list of matched users after your search.
You can click the select button beside the username to chat with that specific user.
